I am currently pursuing my doctoral degree in Mechanical Engineering at The Ohio State University in the department of Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering. Concurrently, I hold the position of Graduate Research Associate at the System, Optimization, and Autonomous Robotics (SOAR) Lab, under the guidance of Dr. Debdipta Goswami.

I completed my MS in Mechanical Engineering from Miami University. There, I was advised by Prof. Kumar Vikram Singh & Prof. Jeong-Hoi Koo. My MS thesis titled Modeling and Experimental Evaluation of Haptic Rendering in Touch Surfaces Using Multifrequency Electrostatic Actuation focused on the generation of vibrotactile haptic feedback in large touch screens using a limited number of vibration actuators with multi-frequency excitations.
